# As a User, Publish Content

The publication can take various forms according your goals:
* PDF document to send as an attachment
* Document to archive to your favourite ECM (Google Drive, Sharepoint or Office 365, NextCloud...) or link in your favourite app (ERP, CRM...)
* Web page to publish on your web site
* LinkedIn post, with carrousel, to publish on your personal or company page
* Insta story, to publish on your insta account
* X post
* Article to post on LinkedIn, Medium, your web site...

## Steps

For each of these publications, you must first define targets. A target is defined from the following informations:
* post URL
* username
* password or token
